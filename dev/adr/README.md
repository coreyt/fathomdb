# ADRs

## Purpose

Accepted, proposed, superseded, or draft architectural decisions.

## Keep here

- ADRs that record durable architectural choices
- amendments to prior ADRs
- future-release ADRs that are still engineering-internal

## Do not keep here

- rough brainstorming notes
- public-facing explanations of architectural choices
- subsystem design details that belong in `dev/design/`

## Canonicality

Canonical for recorded architecture decisions.

## Lifecycle

Entries should be updated intentionally and kept historically traceable.
