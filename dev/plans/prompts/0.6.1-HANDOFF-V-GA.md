## Do not

- Do not edit `release.yml` mid-cycle. The workflow shape is stable
  post-WF-FIX-2; any new workflow defect surfaces in the actual
  release log and gets its own WF-FIX-N slice.
- Do not retry the same dry-run in CI more than once if it fails.
  Run `scripts/release/local-dry-run.sh` first; CI dry-run is a
  final-confirmation tool, not a debug loop.
- Do not skip the rc-bump/ga-bump step before tagging. The tag
  enforcement at `verify-release-gates.sh:74` will reject the tag
  and cost a destructive remote-tag delete to recover.
- Do not invent prompts or restructure plans. V and GA already
  exist; use them.
