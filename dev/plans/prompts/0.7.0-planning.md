# Phase 0.7.0-planning — Perf-only minor release planning slice

You are the **implementer** for the 0.7.0 planning slice. 0.7.0 is a
**perf-only single-thrust minor release** (HITL 2026-05-24). The
release has one goal: bring the AC-012 / AC-013 / AC-019 / AC-020
canonical-runner numbers into a defensible, empirically-pinned
budget by (a) revising the budgets against real measurements and
(b) tuning SQLite + FathomDB engine-side levers to meet them.

This is a **non-code planning slice.** No source changes. Your job
is to produce the planning artifacts on disk (plan doc, ADR drafts,
slice skeletons, AC proposals) so the orchestrator can spawn
per-slice implementers against locked ADRs.

> canonical-identity substrate. Per HITL 2026-05-24 those moved to
> 0.8.0 alongside the Memex knowledge-store / retrieval features
> that consume the substrate (`dev/roadmap/0.8.0.md`). The previous
> version of this prompt is in git history at the commit prior to
> the perf-only rewrite. Do NOT carry substrate / verb / migration
> tasks into 0.7.0 — they belong to 0.8.0 planning.

## 0.7.0 anchor (locked, do not re-scope)

1. **Revise the budgets empirically.** Author
   `dev/adr/ADR-0.7.0-text-query-latency-gates-revised.md` (and
   parallel successor ADRs for AC-013 / AC-019 / AC-020 if
   measurements justify a per-AC revision rather than a single
   omnibus). Budgets are pinned against canonical-runner
   measurements at 1 M corpus on tier-1 x86_64 (`ubuntu-latest`,
   per `dev/adr/ADR-0.6.0-tier1-ci-platforms.md`), **not**
   theoretical "warm-cache" reasoning. The 0.6.1 numbers above are
   the empirical baseline the revised budgets must beat (or, if
   HITL accepts, the empirical baseline the revised budgets ratify).
2. **Tune SQLite + FathomDB.** Engine-side levers to bring the
   measured numbers down to whatever the revised budgets pin. The
   lever menu (decide by measurement, not theory):
   - top-K `LIMIT` cap on the search verb (caller-spec change;
     bounded result-set traversal vs full materialization)
   - `PRAGMA page_cache_size`, `mmap_size`, `temp_store=MEMORY`
   - tokenizer / FTS5 config
   - `SQLITE_CONFIG_PCACHE2` for AC-020 hot-path
   - WAL2 / reader-writer pool split (ADR-class)
   - vendor-SQLite swap (libSQL / sqlite3mc; ADR-class, large
     blast radius — defer if first-tier levers close the gap)

   **The reverted-experiments ledger
   (`dev/notes/performance-whitepaper-notes.md § 5`) is the
   do-not-retry list.** This planning slice MUST NOT propose any
   lever on that ledger. § 7 (untried options ranked) seeds the
   lever menu.
