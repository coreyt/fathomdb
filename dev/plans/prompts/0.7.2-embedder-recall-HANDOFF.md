**The three load-bearing findings of the correction:**
1. **Mean is a non-lever.** Forcing the full-corpus mean on the real engine →
   **0.847**, only **+1.9pp** over the worst mean (drift_cos_before = 1.0, i.e.
   PR-2b had already converged the pin). PR-2a's "fix the mean → 0.945" does not
   transfer to the real path.
2. **candle ≡ HF embeddings** — cos 1.00000, 100% sign-bit agreement → embeddings
   not the cause.
3. **~6pp of the offline→real gap was conservative harness methodology**: −4.4pp
   excluding the self-retrieving target *after* top-10 (64% of synthetic queries
   self-retrieve), −1.4pp body-string GT against 5.6% duplicate bodies. Measured
   the standard ANN way, recall ≈ **0.937**.

**Net:** recall was never broken. The 0.90 floor **holds**. PR-2b/2c solved a
non-problem for recall (possible residual value only as hygiene / genuine
topic-drift safety). The real deliverables are the **harness fix** and the
**EU-8 IR harness**.

## Part 3 — The to-do work, and HOW TO EVALUATE each outcome

For each item: the claim, the reproduction, the acceptance criterion, and the
decision it feeds. **Re-derive D-A/D-B/D-C yourself** (load-bearing); spot-check
the rest.

All real-engine runs are release + feature-gated + AGENT_LONG and take ~1–2 h
(the harness re-embeds the corpus single-threaded for ground truth — a **silent
~25–45 min stretch after the last `EU7_SEED_PROGRESS` and before `seed_done`;
this is NOT a hang, do not kill it**; see the logging TODO in the root-cause doc).
Build once with `--features default-embedder`; warm the embedder cache first.

**D-A — Corrected ANN recall = 0.937 (the claim that kills PR-2a's GO).**
- Run the corrected harness from the `0.7.2-PR-2c-representative-pin` worktree:
  `AGENT_LONG=1 cargo test --release -p fathomdb-engine --features default-embedder --test eu7_real_corpus_ac -- --nocapture`.
- Accept iff: NEW method ≈ **0.937 (CI ~0.913–0.957)** AND OLD method ≈ **0.86**
  (the +7.8pp is purely methodology). Read `measure_recall` in `a601d1c` and
  confirm NEW = retrieve top-(10+1) → drop the query-source doc **before** taking
  10 → match GT **by id/dedup** (not body string). If NEW doesn't reproduce, the
  whole correction is suspect — stop and escalate.

**D-B — Mean is a non-lever = 0.847 (kills the PR-2a premise).**
- Re-run with `EU7_FORCE_FULL_RECOMPUTE=1`. Accept iff recall ≈ **0.847** and the
  log shows `drift_cos_before ≈ 1.0000` (proves the pinned mean already equals the
  full-corpus mean, so PR-2b/2c's mean work cannot move recall).

**D-C — candle ≡ HF (rules out embeddings).**
- `tests/candle_dump.rs` (uncommitted in the PR-2c worktree) dumps candle vectors;
  compare to the saved HF vectors in `dev/research/eu-0/`. Accept iff cos = 1.0 and
  100% sign-bit agreement across the sample (incl. a long >512-token body).

**D-D — IR recall = 0.571, and "don't chase K".**
- Run `eu8_ir_validation.rs` (from the `5658d39` worktree): full corpus + 200
  chains + 301 labeled queries. Accept iff overall recall@10 ≈ **0.571**, MRR ≈
  0.69, and the relation cut holds (action_from ~0.81 high … contradicts ~0.29
  low). Reading: ANN 0.937 ≫ IR 0.571 (+37pp) ⇒ by the "fidelity ≥ IR-ceiling +
  15–20% ⇒ index tuning is wasted" rule, **do NOT bump K/`TOP_K_BIT_CANDIDATES`**.
  Note the IR caveats (synthetic task; `contradicts` is the graph's job, not vector
  search; dup-body→id mapping undercounts) — IR 0.571 is a floor on semantic
  quality, not a verdict on the product.

**D-E — Land the harness fix + EU-8 IR harness (recommended regardless).**
- The harness fix corrects the measurement that caused this detour; EU-8 adds the
  orthogonal IR signal (engine bytes unchanged). Evaluate: do they cherry-pick
  cleanly onto main? do existing tests stay green? Land plan in the decision memo.

**D-F — PR-2c keep/shelve (rec: SHELVE).** Recall benefit ~0; costs = reopening the
