## Hard constraints

- **HITL approval required before GA tag push.**
- **Push `main` FIRST, then tag.** Same lesson as RC1.
- **Single annotated tag at the GA-bump SHA.** Do not chain
  bumps.
- **No tag re-use.** If GA partial-publishes, surface — RC2 is
  unusable as a GA tag.
- **No retry on `verify-release-gates` failure referencing the
  ref shape** — surface immediately.
- **No workflow edits at GA tag time.** WF-FIX is a separate
  slice; if a defect surfaces, the GA aborts and re-cuts after
  the WF-FIX lands.
- **No release-notes edits during GA.** Already finalized at
  DOCS / BUMP.
- **Axis-E stays at 0.6.0.** No trait-surface change in 0.7.0
  per Wake `d-001`.
