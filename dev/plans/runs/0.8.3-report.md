in-footprint *recall* levers (embedder — ruled out by Slice 15a NO-SWAP; PRF; D2/15b) are unlikely to

memory-formation layer** (the design's pre-registered fork). The **$0 gap-decomposition probe**
(`runs/0.8.3-gap-decomposition-probe-PROPOSAL.md`, HITL-gated) is the decisive next experiment to size
retrieval-vs-answer-formation before investing.
