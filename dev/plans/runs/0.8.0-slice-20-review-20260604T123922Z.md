### Finding (verbatim)

- **[P1] Avoid quadratic scans for active batch edges** —
  `src/rust/crates/fathomdb-engine/src/lib.rs:5878-5879`.
  For batches containing many edges with `logical_id`, each edge scans the entire
  remaining batch (`batch[i+1..].iter().any(...)`) to detect in-batch supersession.
  A legitimate bulk write of N unique logical edges therefore performs O(N²)
  comparisons while holding the single-writer transaction, potentially stalling
  ingestion. **Precompute the final active `(logical_id, kind)` occurrences in one
  pass instead** (→ O(N)).
