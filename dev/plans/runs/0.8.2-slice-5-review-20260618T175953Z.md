### 1. [P1] fused_rerank reranks the WRONG pool — m1_baseline.py:430
The `fused_rerank` arm calls `Engine.search(rerank_depth=K)`, which CE-reranks the **engine's own
production pool** (text-branch-only — the engine dense arm is inert in this build — and capped at the
engine search limit), NOT the in-harness `fused(bm25+dense)` pool. So `fused_rerank=0.314` is
apples-to-oranges and **does NOT establish that the CE hurts multi-hop**. Correctly reranking the fused
pool needs a **standalone rerank API** over an arbitrary passage list (the engine CE is only reachable
inside `search()`).

### 2. [P2] inverted-U power shape mis-centered — m1_power_sim.py:66

inflating that shape's P(GO). The **flat-positive** gating shape (P(GO)≈0.6) is the load-bearing one and
is far less affected, but the shapes aren't comparable as reported.
