## Headline: ALPHA (CE-blend weight) is the dominant lever; production α=0.3 is under-tuned

Pooled, pool_n=50:

| α | r@1 | r@3 | r@5 | r@10 | r@20 | MRR | full-gold-rank |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0 (baseline / no rerank) | 0.018 | 0.167 | 0.358 | 0.540 | 0.657 | 0.290 | 20.06 |
| **0.3 (PRODUCTION)** | 0.036 | 0.221 | 0.398 | 0.548 | 0.660 | 0.347 | 19.69 |
| 0.5 | 0.083 | 0.239 | 0.404 | 0.548 | 0.660 | 0.424 | 19.54 |
| 0.7 | 0.092 | 0.261 | 0.404 | 0.551 | 0.660 | 0.448 | 19.44 |
| **1.0 (pure CE)** | **0.140** | **0.328** | 0.398 | 0.498 | 0.629 | **0.589** | 20.27 |

**Mechanism (confirms the gap-decomposition):** higher α concentrates gold into the TOP ranks
(r@1 ×3.9 vs production, MRR +70%) while **Recall@10/20 stay ~flat** — recall@K is blind to the
reorder, but the answerer (top-K context) sees the cleaner, gold-first order. This is the precise
channel by which the accuracy arm gained +0.0495 from ordering alone.

**Tradeoff at the wide pool:** pure CE (α=1.0) at pool_n=50 *drops* r@10 (0.548→0.498) — CE-confident
distractors displace base-favored gold out of the top-10. Mitigated by a **narrow pool**.

concentration (MRR 0.587, r@5 0.409), and captures ALL gold by rank ~7 (vs ~20 for production).

## Read & recommendation
- The single biggest, infra-free lever is **raising α** (CE weight). Production 0.3 captures only ~⅓
  of the available top-rank lift; the CE itself discriminates well (toy: ce_norm 1.0 vs 0.0).
- α is hardcoded in `fathomdb-engine::ce_rerank`. A Python-side **reblend adapter** (extract ce_norm
  via score=0, reorder at chosen α/pool_n) lets the next **priced accuracy arm** test α=1.0 with NO
  Rust rebuild — same harness as `rerank_accuracy_run`.
- **Next priced arm:** α=1.0, pool_n=10 (primary) vs production α=0.3 baseline → answer accuracy vs
  Mem0. MMR / distractor-suppression stacks ON TOP if the α-retune alone is still short of parity.
- Caveat: recall/MRR are a $0 PROXY; the priced arm re-validates on answer accuracy (the real gate).
