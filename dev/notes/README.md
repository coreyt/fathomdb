# Notes

## Purpose

Supporting internal notes that retain value but are not independently canonical
requirements, ADRs, or design docs.

## Keep here

- historical indices
- audits or reviews that inform current work
- supporting analysis and context documents

## Do not keep here

- accepted requirements or ADRs
- public-facing guidance
- disposable scratch work

## Canonicality

Non-canonical unless another document explicitly cites a note as the source of
record for a narrow point.

## Lifecycle

Notes may be reorganized, promoted, or retired as they age.
